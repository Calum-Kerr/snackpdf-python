How do I merge multiple PDF files?

Simply upload your PDF files, arrange them in the desired order, and click merge. Our tool will combine them into a single PDF document while preserving formatting.

Yes, you can drag and drop files to reorder them before merging. The final PDF will follow the order you specify in the interface.

How many PDF files can I merge at once?

You can merge up to 20 PDF files in a single operation. Each file can be up to 50MB in size for optimal performance.

Will merging PDFs affect quality or formatting?
