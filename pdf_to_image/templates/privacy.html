Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2025

Introduction

At SnackPDF, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our free PDF tools and services.

Information We Collect

Files You Upload

When you use our PDF tools, you may upload files for processing. These files are:

  • Processed temporarily on our servers
  • Automatically deleted within 24 hours
  • Never stored permanently or shared with third parties
  • Processed using secure, encrypted connections

Technical Information

We automatically collect certain technical information to improve our services:

  • IP address (anonymized for analytics)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Process your PDF files and provide our services
  • Improve our website performance and user experience
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance our tools
  • Ensure the security and stability of our platform
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: All file uploads use HTTPS encryption
  • Temporary Processing: Files are processed in memory when possible
  • Automatic Deletion: All uploaded files are deleted within 24 hours
  • Secure Servers: Our servers are hosted in secure data centers
  • No Registration: We don't require accounts, reducing data exposure

Third-Party Services

Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to show you relevant ads. You can learn more about Google's privacy practices at Google Privacy Policy.

Analytics

We use analytics services to understand how our website is used. These services may collect anonymized data about your visit, including pages viewed and time spent on our site.

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Essential website functionality
  • Analytics and performance monitoring
  • Advertising (through Google AdSense)

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect
  • Request deletion of your data (though we automatically delete files)
  • Opt out of analytics tracking
  • Control cookie preferences

Data Retention

We retain data for the shortest time necessary:

  • Uploaded Files: Deleted within 24 hours
  • Analytics Data: Anonymized and retained for up to 26 months
  • Server Logs: Retained for up to 30 days for security purposes

International Data Transfers

Our services are operated from the United Kingdom (Edinburgh, Scotland). If you access our services from outside the UK, your data may be transferred to and processed in the UK, which provides adequate data protection under applicable laws.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, you can contact us:

  • Location: Edinburgh, Scotland, United Kingdom
  • Website: www.snackpdf.com

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), our legal basis for processing your data includes:

  • Legitimate Interest: To provide and improve our services
  • Consent: For analytics and advertising cookies (where required)
  • Contract Performance: To process your files as requested
